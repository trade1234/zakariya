About Us

Premium Ethiopian Coffee Exporter Since 2012

Zakariya Raboye Turi Import Export Company was established in 2012 as a family business with deep roots in Ethiopia's rich coffee heritage. What began as a small operation driven by passion for sharing Ethiopia's exceptional coffee with the world has grown into a respected export company, while maintaining the personal touch and dedication of our family values.

Our founder, Zakariya Raboye, grew up surrounded by Ethiopia's coffee culture and developed a vision to connect small-scale farmers with international markets, ensuring fair compensation while delivering the highest quality beans. Today, our extended family continues this mission, combining traditional knowledge with modern export practices.
