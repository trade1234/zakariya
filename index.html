Understanding Coffee Types

Green Coffee

The Journey Begins: Green Coffee

Green coffee refers to unroasted coffee beans, the raw seeds of the coffee cherry. Ethiopia's diverse microclimates produce beans with distinctive flavor profiles based on altitude, soil, and variety.

Roasted Coffee
Transformation Roast Profiles

The Art of Roasting

Roasting transforms green coffee beans, developing their flavors and aromas. Ethiopian beans can be roasted to various levels—light roasts preserve floral notes, while dark roasts highlight chocolate and spice notes.

Brewing Methods
Preparation Extraction

From Bean to Cup

Ethiopian coffee shines through various brewing methods. Traditional ceremonies use a clay pot called a jebena, while modern methods include pour-over, espresso, and cold brew—each revealing different aspects of Ethiopia's complex coffee profiles.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Ethiopian coffee is renowned for its exceptional diversity of flavors, ranging from fruity and floral to wine-like and chocolatey. As the birthplace of coffee, Ethiopia offers unique heirloom varieties grown at high altitudes in ideal conditions, resulting in complex and distinctive flavor profiles not found anywhere else in the world.

We maintain strict quality control throughout the entire process. This includes careful selection of coffee cherries, proper processing methods, meticulous sorting, and rigorous cupping evaluations. Our team of experts regularly visits farms to ensure best practices are followed, and we conduct multiple quality checks before export.

Our minimum order quantity is typically one container (approximately 19.2 metric tons or 320 bags of 60kg). However, we also offer smaller quantities for specialty coffee buyers and new partnerships. Please contact us directly to discuss your specific requirements.

We work directly with farmers and cooperatives, paying premium prices for quality coffee. We invest in training programs, provide access to better equipment, and help implement sustainable farming practices. Our commitment extends to community development projects including education and healthcare initiatives.

We offer various certifications including Organic, Fair Trade, Rainforest Alliance, and UTZ. We can also work with clients to obtain specific certifications required for their markets. All our coffees come with standard quality certificates and full traceability documentation.

Shipping times vary depending on the destination. Typically, it takes 4-6 weeks from order confirmation to delivery at your port. We handle all export documentation and can arrange shipping through our established logistics partners to ensure timely and secure delivery to any global destination.
